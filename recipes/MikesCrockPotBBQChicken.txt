
-----------------
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
3/4 - 1 cup sweet baby ray's
1/2 cup dijon mustard
1/4 cup balsamic vinagrette
1/4 cup worchestershire sauce

pepper, garlic salt, cumin, chicken rub seasoning ("Sylvia's")

Toss chicken in crock pot. In a bowl, combine ingredients to make sauce. Pour over chicken. Cook LOW for 8 hours. Drain sauce. Shred chicken. Serve french-dip style on toasted roll.

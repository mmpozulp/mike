
-----------------
1/2 of dry quinoa
1 clove of garlic chopped
2 tsp olive oil
1/2 an onion diced
1/2 a container of mushrooms sliced
1/2 cup of broth
1/4 cup of water
1/4 cup of white wine
1 Tbs of sour cream
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh sage chopped (optional)

Add olive oil to the pot and saute garlic until it starts to brown. Add dry quinoa and toast for 2 minutes. Stir often. Add diced onions, mushrooms, broth, water, and wine. Bring to a boil and then simmer on low. Add salt and pepper and cook for 20-25 minutes. Stir every few minutes.The quinoa should turn clearish when cooked. Once cooked turn off stove and remove pot from heat. Add sour cream and sage and mix.

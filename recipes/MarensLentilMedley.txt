
-----------------
Maren's Lentil Medley

1 medium yellow onion, chopped
2 bags spinach, chopped
1 head garlic, chopped
1 bag green peas
2 cups wild rice
1 can seasoned lentils

grape seed oil
pepper medley
red pepper flakes
garam masala
curry powder

Rinse and cook rice. In a large sauce pan sauté garlic then onion in oil. Lower heat and add spinach then lentils and green peas. Season. Serve over rice.

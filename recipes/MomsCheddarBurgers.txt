
-----------------
1 lb ground sirloin
4 slices colby jack cheese
shredded cheddar cheese
2 tbs worchtershire sauce
hamburger buns
condiments (onion, pickles, mustard, relish)

In a large bowl, combine ground sirloin with shredded cheddar and worchtershire sauce. Mold into four, thin, quarter-pound burgers. Grill for 10 mins with flip in middle, or broil at 550 for 6 minutes. Turn off heat then lay on cheese slice.

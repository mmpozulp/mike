
-----------------
2 lbs ground turkey (6% fat)
4 green onions
2 celery stalks
1.5 cups Italian parsley
1 cup grated gouda/parm cheese
6 cloves fresh garlic
2 eggs
2 cups breadcrumbs

cumin
poultry seasoning
old bay

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Chop onions, celery, parsley, garlic. Combine ingredients in large bowl. Season to taste. Form 1-in. diameter meatballs. Cook covered (12 min) in large pirex glass dish and then broil uncovered (3 min) to crisp tops.

Ingredients:
sweet potato
onion
whole garlic cloves
beets (all colors if you can find them)
parsnips, turnip, carrot can also be used
olive oil
kosher salt
pepper
rosemary
2 lemons

1. Cut into one inch pieces
2. Massage with ample olive oil
3. Add kosher salt, pepper, and rosemary to taste. Massage spices in
4. Roast 40-45 minutes
5. Stir ample lemon juice into vegetables straight out of the oven

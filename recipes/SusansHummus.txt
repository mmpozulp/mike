Hummus

2 garlic cloves, cut into very small pieces

2   15 oz. cans of garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed

2/3 cup of tahini (roasted, NOT raw)

1/3 cup lemon juice (about 2 lemons)

1/3 cup olive oil

1 t. salt

1/2 t. Tabasco sauce (or to taste)

water (less than 1/2 cup)

Put all of the ingredients except the water in a food processor or blender. Blend until smooth. Gradually add the water until you get the consistency you want.

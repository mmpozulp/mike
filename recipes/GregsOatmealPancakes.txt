Greg's Oatmeal Pancakes

Oatmeal: 3 1/4 Cup
Four: 1 Cup
Sugar: 1/4 Cup
Baking Powder: 2 Tsp
Baking Soda: 2 Tsp
Salt: 1/2 Tsp
Cinnamon: 1 Tsp
Melted Butter: 1/4 lb
Eggs: 6
Buttermilk: 1 qt.

Mix eggs and buttermilk, add to dry ingredients mix medium speed for 2 minutes, refrigerate overnight.

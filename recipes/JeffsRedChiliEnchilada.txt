Ingredients:
3 cans Las Palmas red chili sauce
1 pack 4 inch corn tortillas (50 is plenty)
1 large onion
32 oz shredded cheese

1. Heat red chili sauce to gentle boil.
2. Dip tortilla in sauce for 2 seconds using tongs
3. Layer tortillas to completely cover baking dish by
   slicing tortilla in half or quarter to fill in edges.
   Follow by finely chopped onion and cheese. Repeat
   4 times or until dish is filled to the top.
4. Pour extra chili sauce into dish corners so that they
   will not burn as easily.
5. Cover in aluminum foil and bake at 350 for 30-45 minutes.
6. Remove foil and broil for 5 minutes until crispy.

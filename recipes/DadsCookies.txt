Oatmeal Raison Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients- makes 24 large cookies (2 cookie sheets with 4 rows of 3 cookies

Butter, softened, 1 stick plus 6 tablespoons
Brown sugar-3/4 cup, firmly packed
Eggs-2
Vanilla-2 teaspoons
White Flour-1 & 1/2 cups
Baking Soda-1 teaspoon
Cinnamon, Ground-2 teaspoons
Quaker Oats Oatmeal-3 cups
Raisins-1 cup
Chocolate Chips, semi-sweet (dark)-1/2 cup

1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar until creamy. Can use a electric mixer or hand mix.
2. Add eggs and vanilla, beat well.
3. Add flour, baking soda and cinnamon.
4. Add oatmeal, raisins and chocolate chips and mix well.
5. Use a teaspoon to drop dough onto ungreased cookie sheet.
6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until light golden brown. Start to check every minute after 8 minutes. Be careful to not overcook as the cookie becomes crispy rather than chewy when overcooked. Surface of cookies need not be completely light golden brown.

Marens Basic Bread Recipe
3 cups wheat flour
1/3 cup white flour
2 tsp salt
4 tsp sugar
10 tsp olive oil
1 packet yeast
2 cup water
mix dry ingredients minus yeast in bowl. mix yeast in warm water and combine with dry ingredients. knead 5 minutes, then olive-oil bowl to reduce sticking and let rest for 5 minutes. Then fold in 4 directions place in olive-oiled bread pan to rise in a warm place for 1 hour 20 minutes. baked at 350 for 45 minutes

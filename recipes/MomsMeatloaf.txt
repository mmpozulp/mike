
-----------------
2 packages lean ground sirloin
1 can healthy tomato soup
2 tbs minced onion
2 big tbs chili sauce
1 tbs worchtershire sauce
paprika
1 tbs mrs. dash garlic & herb
pepper
1 egg
1 slice bread

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a small bowl, crack egg, and add torn 1/4-inch parts of warm (lightly toasted to pick up more egg) bread piece. In a large bowl, combine ground sirloin, eggy-bread, minced onion, chili sauce, worchtershire sauce, mrs. dash garlic & herb, and pepper. Form loaf and sprinkle paprika on top. Bake for 45 minutes. 

Remove from oven, Spoon grease out of pan. Pour tomato soup on top. Bake for 15 minutes.

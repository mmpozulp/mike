
-----------------
4 cans of clams (chopped or minced or both)
6 tsp minced garlic
3 tbs olive oil
ground pepper medley
1 box linguine

Satuee garlic with olive oil in pan on low flame - don't overcook garlic. Add 2 cans of clam juice and pepper, let simmer 15 minutes. Add clams, then heat (about 1 minute).

Cook linguine al dente in pot of water.
